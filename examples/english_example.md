# 英语阅卷示例

## 示例1：语法填空

### 题目
阅读下面短文，在空白处填入1个适当的单词或括号内单词的正确形式。

One of my __1__ (fond) memories of high school is the school music festival. It __2__ (hold) every year in June, and students from all grades participate in it.

### 学生答案
1. fond
2. held

### 阅卷结果

**【评分结果】**
得分：0.75分（满分1.5分×2=3分）

**【逐空分析】**

**第1空**：fond → **0分**
- 正确答案：fondest
- 错误原因：需要用形容词最高级形式，"one of my fondest memories"
- 知识要点：one of + the/形容词性物主代词 + 形容词最高级 + 名词复数

**第2空**：held → **0.75分**
- 正确答案：is held
- 错误原因：缺少助动词is，被动语态结构不完整
- 部分得分原因：held形式正确，体现了被动含义

**【改进建议】**
1. 注意"one of + 最高级"结构
2. 被动语态要完整：be + 过去分词
3. 通读全文，注意时态和语态的呼应

## 示例2：书面表达

### 题目
（25分）假定你是李华，你校英文报"Teens"栏目正策划一期题为"Challenging Yourself"的访谈节目。请给外教Ms. Jenkins写一封邮件，邀请她做一次访谈。

内容包括：
1. 节目介绍；
2. 访谈的时间和话题。

注意：1. 词数100左右；2. 可以适当增加细节，以使行文连贯。

### 学生作文

Dear Ms. Jenkins,

I am Li Hua. I am writing to invite you to our school English newspaper "Teens". We have a program about "Challenging Yourself". We want you to come.

The time is next Friday afternoon at 3 o'clock. We will talk about how to challenge yourself in study and life. I think it is very interesting.

I hope you can come. I am looking forward to your reply.

Yours,
Li Hua

### 阅卷结果

**【评分结果】**
得分：15分（满分25分）

**【档次评定】**
第三档（15-21分：基本完成任务）

**【评分维度】**

| 维度 | 得分 | 评价 |
|------|------|------|
| 内容要点 | 6分/9分 | 基本要点齐全，但缺少节目详细介绍 |
| 词汇语法 | 4分/9分 | 词汇简单，语法有少量错误 |
| 篇章结构 | 3分/4分 | 结构完整，但衔接不够自然 |
| 语言得体 | 2分/3分 | 语气基本恰当，但过于简单 |

**【主要问题】**

1. **词汇简单（扣3分）**
   - "want you to come"过于口语化
   - 应改为："we would like to invite you to be our guest"
   - "I think it is very interesting"表达平淡
   - 应改为："Your insights would be invaluable to our students"

2. **语法错误（扣2分）**
   - "I am writing to invite you to our school English newspaper"
   - 错误：invite sb to sth（语义不当）
   - 应改为：invite you to participate in our program

3. **内容不够充实（扣3分）**
   - 节目介绍过于简单
   - 应补充：节目形式、受众、目的等
   - 缺少礼貌客套语

**【改进建议】**

1. **增加词汇多样性**
   - 使用更正式的表达
   - 运用连接词（Furthermore, Additionally, Therefore）

2. **丰富句式结构**
   - 使用复合句和从句
   - 适当使用被动语态

3. **充实内容**
   - 详细介绍节目背景和目的
   - 说明选择外教的原因
   - 表达对对方的尊重

**【参考范文】**

Dear Ms. Jenkins,

I am Li Hua, a student from Class 3, Grade 2. On behalf of our school English newspaper "Teens", I am writing to cordially invite you to be a guest on our upcoming interview program titled "Challenging Yourself".

"Challenging Yourself" is a newly launched program aimed at inspiring students to step out of their comfort zones and embrace challenges. Each episode features an inspiring figure who shares their personal stories of overcoming difficulties. Given your rich experience in education and your own journey of adapting to a new culture, we believe your insights would be tremendously valuable to our audience.

The interview is scheduled for next Friday, June 15th, from 3:00 p.m. to 4:00 p.m. in the school lecture hall. We would like to discuss topics such as how to overcome language barriers and cultural differences, as well as strategies for maintaining a positive mindset when facing challenges.

We would be honored if you could accept our invitation. Please let us know your availability at your earliest convenience.

Yours sincerely,
Li Hua
