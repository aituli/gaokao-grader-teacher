## 📖 原文及题目

### 阅读原文

**Bamboo Drifting: A Dance on Water**

In the picture, a girl is standing on a bamboo pole (竹竿) on the river and dancing beautifully. It's not a scene from a movie, but a form of intangible cultural heritage (非物质文化遗产) — bamboo drifting (独竹漂).

Yang Liu, 24, is an inheritor (传承人) of bamboo drifting. She started to learn it from her grandmother when she was 7. **As she was weak in health as a child, her grandmother wanted her to get into better shape through this sport.** Yang threw herself into practicing bamboo drifting all year round, even in the winter cold and summer heat.

"It was common for me to fall into the water when I started practicing," recalled Yang, adding that **it took her three years to learn the basic skills** of bamboo drifting.

**In order to acquire more bamboo drifting skills,** Yang had to practice countless difficult movements on a bamboo pole that was 9 m in length and 15 cm in diameter (直径), and stand all the physical pain that came with it. Through years of hard training, **Yang now considers the bamboo pole as her "best friend".**

At one point, inspired by her grandmother, Yang began to combine dance with bamboo drifting. "My grandmother came up with an idea. She said that I had been practicing bamboo drifting since I was a child, so why not try to combine dance with the skill? It might be something different," the young woman explained.

Yang's hard work gradually paid off when she was able to perfectly perform dancing with the traditional bamboo drifting skills. **"I hope that more people can know about bamboo drifting and enjoy this form of art," said Yang.**

**22. What's the main idea of the text?**

A. The ways to plan for a travel.  
B. The ways of building a house.  
C. The skills of controlling yourself.  
D. The importance and steps of making a plan.

**学生答案：D** ⚠️ **存疑**

**深度解析**：

学生选了D，但D选项"制定计划的重要性和步骤"与文章内容**不符**。

**文章实际主旨**：
- 介绍了独竹漂这项非物质文化遗产
- 讲述了杨柳学习独竹漂的经历
- 展示了她将舞蹈与独竹漂结合的创新

**选项分析**：
- ❌ A（旅行计划方法）- 无关
- ❌ B（建房方法）- 无关  
- ❌ C（自我控制技巧）- 偏离
- ⚠️ D（制定计划）- **与文章主旨不符**

**⚠️ 可能的正确答案**：如果选项有"The inheritance and innovation of bamboo drifting"（独竹漂的传承与创新）会更准确。建议确认标准答案。

**23. Why did Yang's grandmother let her learn bamboo drifting?**

A. Because it could help Yang build up her body.  
B. Because Yang was very talented for it.  
C. Because it was the traditional skill of Yang's family.  
D. Because Yang wanted to know about the intangible cultural heritage.

**学生答案：A** ✅ **正确**

**原文依据**：
> "As she was weak in health as a child, her grandmother wanted her to **get into better shape** through this sport."

**关键句解析**：
- "weak in health" = 身体虚弱
- "get into better shape" = 增强体质/改善身体状况
- "through this sport" = 通过这项运动

**选项对比**：
- ✅ **A. build up her body**（增强体质）
  - "build up her body" ≈ "get into better shape"（同义替换）
  - **正确！**

- ❌ B（很有天赋）- 文中未提及
- ❌ C（家族传统）- 文中说祖母是传承人，但没说这是家族传统
- ❌ D（想了解非遗）- 这是后来的想法，不是祖母的初衷

**评分**：✅ **2分/2分**

**语言点**：
- `build up` = 增强、建立
- `get into shape` = 健身、保持体型
- `weak in health` = 身体虚弱

**24. What does the underlined word "acquire" mean in English?**

A. sell  
B. collect  
C. get  
D. refuse

**学生答案：C** ✅ **正确**

**原文定位**：
> "In order to **acquire** more bamboo drifting skills, Yang had to practice countless difficult movements..."

**上下文语境**：
- "acquire more skills" = 获得更多的技能
- 后面说"练习无数困难动作"→ 是为了"获得/掌握"技能

**选项分析**：
- ❌ A. sell（卖）- 语义相反
- ❌ B. collect（收集）- 技能不能"收集"
- ✅ **C. get（获得）** - 同义词替换
- ❌ D. refuse（拒绝）- 语义相反

**评分**：✅ **2分/2分**

**词汇拓展**：
- `acquire` = 获得、习得（较正式）
- `acquire knowledge/skills` = 获得知识/技能
- 近义词：obtain, gain, learn, master

**易错提醒**：
> 注意区分 `acquire`（获得）和 `require`（要求），拼写相近但含义不同。

**25. Which of the following is TRUE according to the passage?**

A. Yang is a little tired of bamboo drifting now.  
B. Yang hopes bamboo drifting can be known by more people.  
C. Yang used to stop practicing because it was too cold in winter.  
D. Yang spent 3 months learning the basic skills of bamboo drifting.

**学生答案：B** ✅ **正确**

**选项逐一验证**：

**A. Yang is a little tired of bamboo drifting now.**（厌倦了）
- ❌ **错误**
- 原文："Yang now **considers** the bamboo pole as her 'best friend'."
- 她把竹竿视为"最好的朋友"→ 说明热爱，不是厌倦

**B. Yang hopes bamboo drifting can be known by more people.**（希望更多人了解）
- ✅ **正确**
- 原文最后一句："I hope that **more people can know about** bamboo drifting and enjoy this form of art."
- 完全对应！

**C. Yang used to stop practicing because it was too cold in winter.**（冬天太冷停止练习）
- ❌ **错误**
- 原文："Yang **threw herself into** practicing bamboo drifting **all year round**, even in the winter cold and summer heat."
- "all year round" = 全年无休
- "threw herself into" = 全身心投入

**D. Yang spent 3 months learning the basic skills.**（3个月学会基础）
- ❌ **错误**（常见陷阱！）
- 原文："it took her **three years** to learn the **basic** skills"
- 是 **3年**（years），不是3个月！

**评分**：✅ **2分/2分**

**关键语言点**：
- `all year round` = 全年、一年到头
- `throw oneself into` = 全身心投入
- `know about` = 了解

**陷阱提醒**：
> 注意时间单位陷阱！years vs months 是阅读理解常见考点。

**26. Which word can be used to describe Yang's grandmother?**

A. Hard-working.  
B. Helpful.  
C. Kind-hearted.  
D. Creative.

**学生答案：D** ✅ **正确**

**原文证据**：

祖母的建议：
> "My grandmother **came up with an idea**. She said that I had been practicing bamboo drifting since I was a child, so **why not try to combine dance with the skill?**"

杨柳的实践：
> "Yang began to **combine dance with bamboo drifting**."

**选项分析**：

- A. Hard-working（勤奋的）
  - 杨柳确实勤奋，但题目问的是"grandmother"（祖母），不是杨柳

- B. Helpful（乐于助人的）
  - 未体现

- C. Kind-hearted（心地善良的）
  - 虽然可能，但不是最突出的特点

- ✅ **D. Creative（有创造力的）**
  - **正确！**
  - "came up with an idea" = 想出一个主意
  - "combine dance with the skill" = 将舞蹈与技艺结合
  - 这是典型的**创新思维**！

**评分**：✅ **2分/2分**

**语言点拓展**：
- `come up with` = 想出（主意、办法）
- `combine A with B` = 将A与B结合
- `creative` = 有创造力的（名词：creativity 创造力）
- `innovative` = 创新的（同义词）

## 四、文化拓展：独竹漂 (Bamboo Drifting)

### 什么是独竹漂？

**独竹漂**是中国贵州遵义的传统民间技艺，被誉为"一苇渡江"、"水上芭蕾"。

**技艺特点**：
- 表演者站在一根直径约15cm的竹竿上
- 手持一根细竹竿作为桨划水
- 同时做出各种优美动作，甚至舞蹈
- 要求极高的平衡能力和核心力量

**历史渊源**：
- 起源于秦汉时期，最初是交通方式
- 后来发展为民间表演艺术
- 2009年列入贵州省非物质文化遗产名录

**现代发展**：
- 杨柳等传承人将现代舞蹈融入传统技艺
- 在国内外多次展演，获得广泛赞誉
- 成为展示中国传统文化的重要窗口

**英文介绍**：
> Bamboo drifting is a traditional Chinese folk art originated in Zunyi, Guizhou. Performers stand on a bamboo pole and use another pole as an oar to glide on water while performing elegant movements. It requires excellent balance and has been listed as an intangible cultural heritage.

## 五、阅读理解技巧总结

### 1. 主旨大意题策略
- 通读全文，抓住首段和尾段
- 注意高频词和重复出现的主题
- 排除过于具体或偏离主题的选项

### 2. 细节理解题策略
- 题干关键词定位原文
- 注意同义替换（如get into shape = build up body）
- 警惕时间、数字的陷阱（years vs months）

### 3. 词义猜测题策略
- 看上下文语境
- 利用逻辑关系（因果、转折、并列）
- 代入法验证

### 4. 推理判断题策略
- 区分"事实"与"观点"
- 注意作者态度和语气
- 不要过度推理

### 5. 人物性格题策略
- 找人物的语言、行为描写
- 注意其他人物的评价
- 区分主要人物和次要人物
